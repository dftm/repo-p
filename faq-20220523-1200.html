How do I sign up for Persado Academy? 

You can sign up for Persado Academy by entering your business email address and name. You will also set a password for login.

How do I sign into Persado Academy? 

Visit the Academy any time at https://academy.persado.com/. You can then sign in with your business email address and password.

How do I change my password? 

After logging in, click your profile icon in the upper righthand corner and then click ‘My Profile.’ From here, you can set a new password, add a profile picture, and see your registration history. 

What if I forget my password?

On the sign in page, please click ‘Forgot Password?’ Then, enter your business email address so we can send you a password reset link.

Why can’t I access my badge? 

Upon completion of the learning path, you’ll receive an email from Credly, our credentialing platform. If you already have an account with Credly, you can simply sign in to access your badge. In order to access your badge, you must create an account with Credly. There is no charge. 

Where can I share my badge? 

You can share your badge on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, and Zip Recruiter. For more information on how to share your badge, please reference this article from Credly.

What is my badge? 

Your badge is a digital credential that verifies your completion of a learning path and its associated courses. Your badge can be shared on social media as a part of your resume to indicate your knowledge in a particular Persado area. 

How long does my badge last? 

Badges are valid for two years from the date of receipt. 

What is Persado Academy about?

Persado Academy is Persado’s official self-service learning platform. Its goals are to enable our customers on best practices for using our products and services, and to build thought leadership around marketing AI.

What types of materials are on the Persado Academy website? 

  • Tutorials - Brief videos showing you how to accomplish particular tasks in Persado Portal
  • Video Courses - On-demand education of key Persado topics and themes, like Experiments and Variants, taught by Persado thought leaders
  • PDFs and Articles - Written materials on a variety of topics for you to learn at your own pace
  • Learning Paths - Curated courses and supporting materials to help you master particular subjects
  • Final Exams and Certifications - Summative quizzes following learning paths that, when successfully passed, result in certifications you can share on social media.

Who do I contact about technical issues?

Please contact academy@persado.com with any technical issues or questions. 

Why do I see limited content on my home page?

Access to content for courses is based upon your relationship with Persado. Guests have limited access to content at this time.
